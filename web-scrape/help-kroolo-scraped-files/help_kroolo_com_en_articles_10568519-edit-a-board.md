## **1. Locate Your Board**
Find the board you want to edit in your **list of boards**.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910219/683573306a07c1f8525801e6ee54/a9b37c52-d261-40ca-ab75-2a601c8e94d3.png?expires=1747842300&signature=bc59beb4db4e0bc16aab810e3ad9e877957abf72fe54bd940f1d12653b7ad513&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNeUPMW1HO4zYBcrZelS9rhF4vHB45uHf7dtx8THiiibKadAhzL%0AVvIK5G4W0EEbyoUWdNw%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910219/683573306a07c1f8525801e6ee54/a9b37c52-d261-40ca-ab75-2a601c8e94d3.png?expires=1747842300&signature=bc59beb4db4e0bc16aab810e3ad9e877957abf72fe54bd940f1d12653b7ad513&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNeUPMW1HO4zYBcrZelS9rhF4vHB45uHf7dtx8THiiibKadAhzL%0AVvIK5G4W0EEbyoUWdNw%3D%0A)
## **2. Access Edit Options**
Click the **three dots** next to the board to open more options.
[![](https://kroolo-e0b70269b6e2.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910293/3e3c852da79cf6b3502eafc94fd2/c5d2a7c1-4628-4431-886e-247208fbf950.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=470dc67371adecc47aae24d50e9ad16ccb87910e7bc02b6df8cd85dfec26c141&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNWWvMW1HO4zXs4uAa3EhhhZhRSrrHk3qyuvyUUGi%2FsXAULtN1x%0A1qzmRrshxTIHpOZ3jo8%3D%0A)](https://kroolo-e0b70269b6e2.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910293/3e3c852da79cf6b3502eafc94fd2/c5d2a7c1-4628-4431-886e-247208fbf950.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=470dc67371adecc47aae24d50e9ad16ccb87910e7bc02b6df8cd85dfec26c141&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNWWvMW1HO4zXs4uAa3EhhhZhRSrrHk3qyuvyUUGi%2FsXAULtN1x%0A1qzmRrshxTIHpOZ3jo8%3D%0A)
## **3. Select "Edit Board"**
Choose **Edit Board** from the dropdown menu.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910222/78a01707e6fdf2f07a7b16cc8b31/7b0c46eb-8949-480a-a828-fd4667e31ab1.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=18142691ad3add138d345753404ed9552e6e59df31821540fede295a48e21485&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdW%2FMW1HO4zZ2eVQwFd%2B8SD8y7SJQjiBE86fCR%2BHBxm1sQnL%2FK%0AlWiNQ60e7BK9ctxQCBY%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910222/78a01707e6fdf2f07a7b16cc8b31/7b0c46eb-8949-480a-a828-fd4667e31ab1.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=18142691ad3add138d345753404ed9552e6e59df31821540fede295a48e21485&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdW%2FMW1HO4zZ2eVQwFd%2B8SD8y7SJQjiBE86fCR%2BHBxm1sQnL%2FK%0AlWiNQ60e7BK9ctxQCBY%3D%0A)

  * Change the **board icon color** to match your preference.


[![](https://kroolo-e0b70269b6e2.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910295/14c2993371e3cef8e8e11974c3c1/396411b4-b572-43ae-be31-d001a9ab3752.png?expires=1747842300&signature=5b60042dd13f5bb76f426feff6cded5ab52cbdec20c453428d5935f61b0809a5&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNWXPMW1HO4zV3hKA%2F35CYXfVlbvo%2Fek7w7syws442iyuPbLYnr%0A5W6B0rFiqtooZpev438%3D%0A)](https://kroolo-e0b70269b6e2.intercom-attachments-1.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910295/14c2993371e3cef8e8e11974c3c1/396411b4-b572-43ae-be31-d001a9ab3752.png?expires=1747842300&signature=5b60042dd13f5bb76f426feff6cded5ab52cbdec20c453428d5935f61b0809a5&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNWXPMW1HO4zV3hKA%2F35CYXfVlbvo%2Fek7w7syws442iyuPbLYnr%0A5W6B0rFiqtooZpev438%3D%0A)
  * Modify the **board title** as needed. 


[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910228/f632ecc05d0fd676b99693e587c2/81162406-435d-481b-9494-1333c12d7f41.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=f68dcfd361ebcdade17aa6b27a52ba9c793d52e75d5add3381f82c5ec9419f6b&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdUfMW1HO4zVRGER%2FfEwUQyG3Sm1wdCeV1g0BoCM5EIYCozRtm%0AjtqnEmgDoDKOLk%2B3dx8%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910228/f632ecc05d0fd676b99693e587c2/81162406-435d-481b-9494-1333c12d7f41.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=f68dcfd361ebcdade17aa6b27a52ba9c793d52e75d5add3381f82c5ec9419f6b&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdUfMW1HO4zVRGER%2FfEwUQyG3Sm1wdCeV1g0BoCM5EIYCozRtm%0AjtqnEmgDoDKOLk%2B3dx8%3D%0A)
  * Update the **board description** to reflect its purpose. 


[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910220/b35a496a150e38346d213582f77d/2629b7ab-cf29-41f2-869c-c4091d147d54.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=c79fa28e9f8d55d5c9733050902d85561bdbbca021e8b6e4a9d8f61015ff8182&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdWfMW1HO4zXxQWQrhhOhbco6awRjax5FVhs0%2BWYYXdhOYT%2BI1%0A0uU1KzgC4hc6dmlhsWI%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910220/b35a496a150e38346d213582f77d/2629b7ab-cf29-41f2-869c-c4091d147d54.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=c79fa28e9f8d55d5c9733050902d85561bdbbca021e8b6e4a9d8f61015ff8182&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdWfMW1HO4zXxQWQrhhOhbco6awRjax5FVhs0%2BWYYXdhOYT%2BI1%0A0uU1KzgC4hc6dmlhsWI%3D%0A)
## **5. Add Members**
  * Click **Add Members** and enter the **name or email** of the person you want to invite.


[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910224/20c18713ac79af41751cc6ca1ec9/e7f5b223-4a39-40de-bb53-b2f24f26dd8d.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=a307f73074ec3b6164da3ca9bcfb65de4afb4d7dceb54f5a5b142a5a5feace5d&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdXfMW1HO4zQwtBvaAyAgg5HNhbIwX10uH%2BwMP8quhh9dqSYiq%0Aa1ybenlnfeO1%2FhBrWpY%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910224/20c18713ac79af41751cc6ca1ec9/e7f5b223-4a39-40de-bb53-b2f24f26dd8d.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=a307f73074ec3b6164da3ca9bcfb65de4afb4d7dceb54f5a5b142a5a5feace5d&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdXfMW1HO4zQwtBvaAyAgg5HNhbIwX10uH%2BwMP8quhh9dqSYiq%0Aa1ybenlnfeO1%2FhBrWpY%3D%0A)
  * Select the member and adjust their **access settings**. 


[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910229/076fa8b11949159d5cd79b156414/5b55bc27-a6fc-424a-a6bb-fd7ba1eb9d9d.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=c3fa0a7f50b44c3c8cc8c30bc629eb2f1c00b9cc6ccffbb18ad8229f89d3eae8&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdUPMW1HO4zXNobJYgzHtDqKJJ8cl4nhltIgp%2BWTxCDm%2BrtT3v%0AINn816lSzO7hk2mZzYI%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910229/076fa8b11949159d5cd79b156414/5b55bc27-a6fc-424a-a6bb-fd7ba1eb9d9d.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=c3fa0a7f50b44c3c8cc8c30bc629eb2f1c00b9cc6ccffbb18ad8229f89d3eae8&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdUPMW1HO4zXNobJYgzHtDqKJJ8cl4nhltIgp%2BWTxCDm%2BrtT3v%0AINn816lSzO7hk2mZzYI%3D%0A)
  * Click **Send Invite** to add them. 


[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910231/f46e4a0b79526e078c3dd02c5445/fdf5775d-4a9d-462c-b103-59d621879af3.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=fec1cfaa5d0176355b585c389626630794137bebfa731c9728dad3b33c7f8c0e&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNcWPMW1HO4zQ6ZxguE%2Baavqwu944eCWkL%2BPhk9INOypvv77%2BFU%0AzU19iGk4rucXhgQgu6w%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910231/f46e4a0b79526e078c3dd02c5445/fdf5775d-4a9d-462c-b103-59d621879af3.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=fec1cfaa5d0176355b585c389626630794137bebfa731c9728dad3b33c7f8c0e&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNcWPMW1HO4zQ6ZxguE%2Baavqwu944eCWkL%2BPhk9INOypvv77%2BFU%0AzU19iGk4rucXhgQgu6w%3D%0A)
## **6. Share the Board**
Copy the **private link** and share it with members if needed. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910241/aca0a6a50db03e4d027462605827/d2cd4aaa-f620-4309-9e76-345e39af6a62.png?expires=1747842300&signature=d2d263222de011aa6487807cb3e296033b21f7cf6a8edaa27651d7b7c8967a07&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNbWPMW1HO4zXFAIUr38bOrHjuCaigTm%2FutObrP8BEk3%2BAWR2Ml%0AnEECjBMzybCEZfcgDmo%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910241/aca0a6a50db03e4d027462605827/d2cd4aaa-f620-4309-9e76-345e39af6a62.png?expires=1747842300&signature=d2d263222de011aa6487807cb3e296033b21f7cf6a8edaa27651d7b7c8967a07&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNbWPMW1HO4zXFAIUr38bOrHjuCaigTm%2FutObrP8BEk3%2BAWR2Ml%0AnEECjBMzybCEZfcgDmo%3D%0A)
## **7. Adjust Privacy Settings**
Modify the **privacy settings** according to your preferences.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910239/fb545b386f4f9bbc1c44c5b882fb/9046bbd9-404b-4aab-a9c3-1ca821b8ff14.png?expires=1747842300&signature=07ebe3299859adb4967afafe7574b78580428dae0f6ecf8f6ff322d475c809ed&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNcUPMW1HO4zYt6fuwGHj9kbPRE4GneqvfsCsRKjkZPiJiiI4Ev%0AbXSRUUuEbPMU47GnRCQ%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910239/fb545b386f4f9bbc1c44c5b882fb/9046bbd9-404b-4aab-a9c3-1ca821b8ff14.png?expires=1747842300&signature=07ebe3299859adb4967afafe7574b78580428dae0f6ecf8f6ff322d475c809ed&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNcUPMW1HO4zYt6fuwGHj9kbPRE4GneqvfsCsRKjkZPiJiiI4Ev%0AbXSRUUuEbPMU47GnRCQ%3D%0A)
## **8. Save Changes**
Click anywhere outside the popup to **automatically save** your updates.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910226/db13e9e4242f9a2e9aaf7fddf0c7/398e7317-30aa-40d5-8cf1-9fb494bc2dad.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2863c2a715e2e3219f6b1da3a88eb681686d4eadca7401461fe01182c747ab90&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdX%2FMW1HO4zVs2tN5UDUxaUoLONoDiI04gq1K6jYWKuNEDaFlK%0AdULuHO0wJHqmQ3FKEog%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910226/db13e9e4242f9a2e9aaf7fddf0c7/398e7317-30aa-40d5-8cf1-9fb494bc2dad.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2863c2a715e2e3219f6b1da3a88eb681686d4eadca7401461fe01182c747ab90&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdX%2FMW1HO4zVs2tN5UDUxaUoLONoDiI04gq1K6jYWKuNEDaFlK%0AdULuHO0wJHqmQ3FKEog%3D%0A)

Check that the **title, icon, and settings** have been updated correctly. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910223/c1e1527b2bb926c77be2835fe527/8dfe27e0-4d1d-4a35-a4d5-cbea93f089a4.png?expires=1747842300&signature=5c04be87a798c3646e50ed0676661f43d63417c81119e2071bd979aea8af6d7b&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdWvMW1HO4zY22otnc5vQeZyCPxXPzztOF58OlXFv3mOWzkBuw%0Af1bDYUpy4YQ89RYP%2BCs%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1384910223/c1e1527b2bb926c77be2835fe527/8dfe27e0-4d1d-4a35-a4d5-cbea93f089a4.png?expires=1747842300&signature=5c04be87a798c3646e50ed0676661f43d63417c81119e2071bd979aea8af6d7b&req=dSMvEsB%2FnYNdWvMW1HO4zY22otnc5vQeZyCPxXPzztOF58OlXFv3mOWzkBuw%0Af1bDYUpy4YQ89RYP%2BCs%3D%0A)
