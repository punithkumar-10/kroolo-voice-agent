# Create Custom Automations in Projects
Welcome to Kroolo, your smart productivity powerhouse. In this article, you'll learn how to create custom automation in Kroolo to automate task update notifications.
## **1. Open Your Project**
Log in to Kroolo and open the project where you want to set up automation.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324148/329ec8c4fb8a74fdfcb7294781c0/b494a807-1b24-48a7-b7a3-22e95a1466ee.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2045b0944d5a6722150e5cb429f187a6ad565c8416fec1022578b534f75d3dfa&req=dSMnFMp8mYBbUfMW1HO4zX6qSWKBf1acHU7xAqE7xSrhnTqf2zr3AypQgz3%2B%0AlIQGTidg671Ba79Cvk0%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324148/329ec8c4fb8a74fdfcb7294781c0/b494a807-1b24-48a7-b7a3-22e95a1466ee.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2045b0944d5a6722150e5cb429f187a6ad565c8416fec1022578b534f75d3dfa&req=dSMnFMp8mYBbUfMW1HO4zX6qSWKBf1acHU7xAqE7xSrhnTqf2zr3AypQgz3%2B%0AlIQGTidg671Ba79Cvk0%3D%0A)
## **2. Access the Automations Panel**
Locate and click on the **"Automations"** button in your project toolbar.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324147/2eba7f8cb1fddefc08cc1f802226/d469f124-4308-4837-ada4-d461f268dca0.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=9f40a9f423a209e173a2c338d613101bbe2b900a3a8a7640c2a1b01bb197d768&req=dSMnFMp8mYBbXvMW1HO4zeDoWEU5VLre6MY8UWt6BTvhfLsRepUaqFxjklwQ%0ADMrKUAIfofZSax8rpe4%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324147/2eba7f8cb1fddefc08cc1f802226/d469f124-4308-4837-ada4-d461f268dca0.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=9f40a9f423a209e173a2c338d613101bbe2b900a3a8a7640c2a1b01bb197d768&req=dSMnFMp8mYBbXvMW1HO4zeDoWEU5VLre6MY8UWt6BTvhfLsRepUaqFxjklwQ%0ADMrKUAIfofZSax8rpe4%3D%0A)
## **3. Navigate to the Custom Automation Tab**
In the Automations panel, switch to the **"Custom"** tab. This is where you'll set up and define your custom automation.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324165/37746df97e16beb9af0f7e79ced8/cccb65a6-e62a-4938-838d-b25749ead3ed.png?expires=1747842300&signature=2fc0c0bd5f0d4bdd7e5e8c738f0e1f71bedd38cec92b78c62c7a70e2386ab532&req=dSMnFMp8mYBZXPMW1HO4zV28mfXlt7DBw9jFhpyx1AuubeTGzA239hpmE9Yd%0AAtKbCQiVK5ApFe9eHII%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324165/37746df97e16beb9af0f7e79ced8/cccb65a6-e62a-4938-838d-b25749ead3ed.png?expires=1747842300&signature=2fc0c0bd5f0d4bdd7e5e8c738f0e1f71bedd38cec92b78c62c7a70e2386ab532&req=dSMnFMp8mYBZXPMW1HO4zV28mfXlt7DBw9jFhpyx1AuubeTGzA239hpmE9Yd%0AAtKbCQiVK5ApFe9eHII%3D%0A)
## **4. Choose a Condition**
Click on the **drop-down menu** under "Custom." Select a condition that triggers the automation. For this guide, choose **"When a task is updated."**
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324158/6ec6566219d9414bc4f0d1d2008e/85969be7-e0bb-479f-b3f9-d332f77f6b3b.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=93a0a0dd4582e512f7a0f5a15b9b2af1cfa9105f240c1b37cb547051d618d9c4&req=dSMnFMp8mYBaUfMW1HO4zd1XtkzNZxLphw0ZI6bdXbkdcxJaFHo%2BQGmb%2BZGe%0AB7E4MqRozuUbBZZ9Ehk%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324158/6ec6566219d9414bc4f0d1d2008e/85969be7-e0bb-479f-b3f9-d332f77f6b3b.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=93a0a0dd4582e512f7a0f5a15b9b2af1cfa9105f240c1b37cb547051d618d9c4&req=dSMnFMp8mYBaUfMW1HO4zd1XtkzNZxLphw0ZI6bdXbkdcxJaFHo%2BQGmb%2BZGe%0AB7E4MqRozuUbBZZ9Ehk%3D%0A)
## **5. Add the Condition**
Click on the **plus (+) button** to add the selected condition to your automation.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324171/c1d3cb195ad217bf7ee6501358dc/5afcd358-6491-4f94-9d0a-b57c8d1a7564.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=305654e4897965219ace99e6feca4cb00cb6bbbc70d9ff910b442ba8161c6d92&req=dSMnFMp8mYBYWPMW1HO4zUsk7F0MLUuGKYiygnNBucwYuYuHUw930jf2u5rJ%0ALOOxR2eY4LTW0bv4vCw%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324171/c1d3cb195ad217bf7ee6501358dc/5afcd358-6491-4f94-9d0a-b57c8d1a7564.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=305654e4897965219ace99e6feca4cb00cb6bbbc70d9ff910b442ba8161c6d92&req=dSMnFMp8mYBYWPMW1HO4zUsk7F0MLUuGKYiygnNBucwYuYuHUw930jf2u5rJ%0ALOOxR2eY4LTW0bv4vCw%3D%0A)
## **6. Define Additional Conditions (Optional)**
You can add more conditions if needed by repeating the above step. For example, you can set conditions based on deadlines, task priorities, or team members.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324164/c050811a11b22c89c64777f6589f/a304f175-48b0-4b7b-a956-136b2c306638.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=e9f4d8b50d974ec71d81aa919ec974beebbcd065e6e7fc518c7244048b569054&req=dSMnFMp8mYBZXfMW1HO4zSodBY5QWk%2FGjuGFnOrhODwuknVt4k1R7gbWLppS%0AHh2fsKuCVmj7NfUG4xU%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324164/c050811a11b22c89c64777f6589f/a304f175-48b0-4b7b-a956-136b2c306638.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=e9f4d8b50d974ec71d81aa919ec974beebbcd065e6e7fc518c7244048b569054&req=dSMnFMp8mYBZXfMW1HO4zSodBY5QWk%2FGjuGFnOrhODwuknVt4k1R7gbWLppS%0AHh2fsKuCVmj7NfUG4xU%3D%0A)
## **7. Choose an Action**
F choose the action you want to automate from the drop-down menu. Remember, you're not restricted to a single condition or action - add as many as you need.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324155/deaa9eb28a0217c3c060f15716c2/f5b8ff4e-126d-4399-9e72-53a4f9cb8f0a.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2626cb07ec4cc516fd0817c7729306df87547624862cd235d270eda44fc2209e&req=dSMnFMp8mYBaXPMW1HO4zQXQjuyEW4SHIJdDcltrGz6xvDYq5aQIT6LrXUJp%0AFkuoBpK%2FC6TWFdmo9ZE%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324155/deaa9eb28a0217c3c060f15716c2/f5b8ff4e-126d-4399-9e72-53a4f9cb8f0a.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2626cb07ec4cc516fd0817c7729306df87547624862cd235d270eda44fc2209e&req=dSMnFMp8mYBaXPMW1HO4zQXQjuyEW4SHIJdDcltrGz6xvDYq5aQIT6LrXUJp%0AFkuoBpK%2FC6TWFdmo9ZE%3D%0A)
## 8. Trigger Notification to Google Space
In our example, we're choosing **Space** and **Message**. This automation will trigger a notification to Google Space whenever a task is completed.
Click **Save**. Your automation is now ready. Close the pop-up window.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324169/c51178ffd72ae0f73cefa607ec48/2401bdb3-82b0-4338-aac7-4b6a6307bd2b.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=545896533dea81834b6cdaa19b13276a4313fadcf7e6f75277a56403b1e1f989&req=dSMnFMp8mYBZUPMW1HO4zWg08Gc8iuBE17maWEv2Emmhf5zRjF7C3rp5%2B8Bg%0Atkhqn8%2BVIs1Ujxehsao%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324169/c51178ffd72ae0f73cefa607ec48/2401bdb3-82b0-4338-aac7-4b6a6307bd2b.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=545896533dea81834b6cdaa19b13276a4313fadcf7e6f75277a56403b1e1f989&req=dSMnFMp8mYBZUPMW1HO4zWg08Gc8iuBE17maWEv2Emmhf5zRjF7C3rp5%2B8Bg%0Atkhqn8%2BVIs1Ujxehsao%3D%0A)
## 9. **Test Your Automation**
To test your automation, mark a task as **completed.**
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324157/a9e3b7ebb0208e9522ea227e88f5/8e1c6c9c-f37c-4185-aa40-43aa56fc791b.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=be59ce239b0a9306d4b5ddb03a4362944c35b2cc866ec59af9bed1c16ff1c320&req=dSMnFMp8mYBaXvMW1HO4zb%2BJlyKlQrZxUz7D0GRgiVBY39%2FqKnTo95WLMZha%0AreNS1eQ58raOpTNuuNA%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324157/a9e3b7ebb0208e9522ea227e88f5/8e1c6c9c-f37c-4185-aa40-43aa56fc791b.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=be59ce239b0a9306d4b5ddb03a4362944c35b2cc866ec59af9bed1c16ff1c320&req=dSMnFMp8mYBaXvMW1HO4zb%2BJlyKlQrZxUz7D0GRgiVBY39%2FqKnTo95WLMZha%0AreNS1eQ58raOpTNuuNA%3D%0A)
## 10. Go to Google Chat 
Open **Google Chat** and navigate to the Space associated with your project. 
You should see a **"Task Completed"** notification in Google Space, including the name of the task. For example: **"Content Strategy completed."**
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324173/79bd33a8ac1d4d38cb9a876413c7/01901b99-2e5b-430d-931a-1442bc3be374.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=0d772e49918d00fd25f34b1972be0f37f43e1476f6a50a6a802d188d988458eb&req=dSMnFMp8mYBYWvMW1HO4zS0%2Fjff9FZ5Qg2VIe4AZdBA2oKtS1Dce0qH8Eczj%0AAFwyvaZhaTuzmxiFhOs%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302324173/79bd33a8ac1d4d38cb9a876413c7/01901b99-2e5b-430d-931a-1442bc3be374.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=0d772e49918d00fd25f34b1972be0f37f43e1476f6a50a6a802d188d988458eb&req=dSMnFMp8mYBYWvMW1HO4zS0%2Fjff9FZ5Qg2VIe4AZdBA2oKtS1Dce0qH8Eczj%0AAFwyvaZhaTuzmxiFhOs%3D%0A)
Congratulations! You've successfully set up a custom automation in Kroolo.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1302346288/8463d24a710f138bb57531d7f43a/cta+2.png?expires=1747842300&signature=9383fe767b18d709ffb6cc0f2ef27a863f1dfc08f8256ab5cc159bcc29b7d2f8&req=dSMnFMp6m4NXUfMW1HO4zdLjAzGxQT6y3MRHpZwIJdW9tSElkut62BeegtVW%0A8LJvHwwk9JM9qjmTNCQ%3D%0A)](https://kroolo.com/)
