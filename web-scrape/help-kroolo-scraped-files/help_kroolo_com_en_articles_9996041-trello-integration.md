# Trello Integration
Welcome to [Kroolo](https://kroolo.com/), the smart productivity powerhouse. This guide explains how to import projects from Trello into Kroolo.
## üìå Setting Up the Trello Integration
## 1. Go to Your Profile
Click on your profile icon at the top of the Kroolo dashboard.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946900/7727be7e96bed8a93ce4c7f46f48/5992c6f3-0119-4a1b-a1a9-aa3e6bb3821d.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=5bee0d6b1f446331eb37e3866114cee7204886b8d6b7ca4f1debe83c82c5596d&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfWfMW1HO4zbUwSlXF1EiYKLidO%2BCu2m9oLIqqVBjE%2BK0oyuEE%0AhrwjQ5D3JauBM6iY7KA%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946900/7727be7e96bed8a93ce4c7f46f48/5992c6f3-0119-4a1b-a1a9-aa3e6bb3821d.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=5bee0d6b1f446331eb37e3866114cee7204886b8d6b7ca4f1debe83c82c5596d&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfWfMW1HO4zbUwSlXF1EiYKLidO%2BCu2m9oLIqqVBjE%2BK0oyuEE%0AhrwjQ5D3JauBM6iY7KA%3D%0A)
## 2. Open Settings
In the profile menu, select the **[Settings](https://help.kroolo.com/en/collections/10446517-settings)** option. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946911/e6b36eac2e5d98a5c2fa9c03de16/c10b673f-84ae-48cc-aa3c-e840da686145.png?expires=1747842300&signature=b4fc2c47252c3d3208001cacfcf4efb031ec5ab7b3a5b3b68e4a20208db83be1&req=dSIkFsB6m4heWPMW1HO4zRGBU8Cm9CFjZ8eNkgL6AVgDpAgXnxpqwtkO%2B%2FG9%0ALGrktl7K2eIE%2FVz7lzI%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946911/e6b36eac2e5d98a5c2fa9c03de16/c10b673f-84ae-48cc-aa3c-e840da686145.png?expires=1747842300&signature=b4fc2c47252c3d3208001cacfcf4efb031ec5ab7b3a5b3b68e4a20208db83be1&req=dSIkFsB6m4heWPMW1HO4zRGBU8Cm9CFjZ8eNkgL6AVgDpAgXnxpqwtkO%2B%2FG9%0ALGrktl7K2eIE%2FVz7lzI%3D%0A)
## 3. Access Integrations
In the settings menu, navigate to **[Integrations](https://help.kroolo.com/en/collections/9118200-integrations)**. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946920/af5a01b3457770d11b40c35dcaa5/be70791a-019a-4cc6-8f58-b4d3f2f81c2a.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=b3539521899581a53ab595d71955b43910162eabe3958c5d218e6c8c801afbe5&req=dSIkFsB6m4hdWfMW1HO4zaWpMQrWxjT2byWqPgDabjeZUZghd0HldPR23gua%0AkljYsBDOss2WX88nCis%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946920/af5a01b3457770d11b40c35dcaa5/be70791a-019a-4cc6-8f58-b4d3f2f81c2a.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=b3539521899581a53ab595d71955b43910162eabe3958c5d218e6c8c801afbe5&req=dSIkFsB6m4hdWfMW1HO4zaWpMQrWxjT2byWqPgDabjeZUZghd0HldPR23gua%0AkljYsBDOss2WX88nCis%3D%0A)
## 4. Search for Trello
Use the search bar to find **Trello** in the list of integrations. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946919/e52daae859fe91f4bb0cf26ca3ae/2898603f-faba-465a-acb8-6003cab055a1.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2ce8c6c6f79f69126b8dd27b294c84bc49ce2a0b0ba1957930e24b906357d360&req=dSIkFsB6m4heUPMW1HO4zXROkU9QnPlO%2BjWee25vITkUBWXMo8E0PPGNGv54%0AsfH6OlG%2B1wdHM2vkzmE%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946919/e52daae859fe91f4bb0cf26ca3ae/2898603f-faba-465a-acb8-6003cab055a1.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=2ce8c6c6f79f69126b8dd27b294c84bc49ce2a0b0ba1957930e24b906357d360&req=dSIkFsB6m4heUPMW1HO4zXROkU9QnPlO%2BjWee25vITkUBWXMo8E0PPGNGv54%0AsfH6OlG%2B1wdHM2vkzmE%3D%0A)
## 5. Connect Trello
Click on **Connect** next to Trello. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946921/285581f6012dd334334936dbaece/cdc2f102-e2ef-45d1-89bf-2415ca47befe.png?expires=1747842300&signature=f0bb784d76de99b929c53822f4ed8a9d8fc1b0a1757076d23f6ced9d117b215b&req=dSIkFsB6m4hdWPMW1HO4zUPzmREo3IGL%2FPUIGWmwfCLjQbKF01p0E%2BbUyNdm%0A%2FuKEvLtNMRSEnAai5j8%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946921/285581f6012dd334334936dbaece/cdc2f102-e2ef-45d1-89bf-2415ca47befe.png?expires=1747842300&signature=f0bb784d76de99b929c53822f4ed8a9d8fc1b0a1757076d23f6ced9d117b215b&req=dSIkFsB6m4hdWPMW1HO4zUPzmREo3IGL%2FPUIGWmwfCLjQbKF01p0E%2BbUyNdm%0A%2FuKEvLtNMRSEnAai5j8%3D%0A)
## 6. Authorize the Connection
Scroll down to the authorization prompt and click **Allow** to complete the setup.
**Your Trello account is now connected to Kroolo!**
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946918/b84496bcdd3a7d8f47837443b43f/607ccc4d-7970-4f6e-b2bb-4c21833656d9.png?expires=1747842300&signature=3e8aa1addf35edd47f0610d27eca0a893660cdd81eee0dc5b9faaf879e609c74&req=dSIkFsB6m4heUfMW1HO4zXqKPr1KgCyokpm9DLoPbffsDTIvkFI9DMkDtkPn%0AUozKXhVknz85SoANxzM%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946918/b84496bcdd3a7d8f47837443b43f/607ccc4d-7970-4f6e-b2bb-4c21833656d9.png?expires=1747842300&signature=3e8aa1addf35edd47f0610d27eca0a893660cdd81eee0dc5b9faaf879e609c74&req=dSIkFsB6m4heUfMW1HO4zXqKPr1KgCyokpm9DLoPbffsDTIvkFI9DMkDtkPn%0AUozKXhVknz85SoANxzM%3D%0A)
## üìå Importing a Project from Trello
## 1. Select the Trello Board
Navigate to the Trello integration and click on **Select Board**. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946906/54a3eb2e08fc79d450b76a72c836/50ae7c12-0507-45c6-b926-678a8b54bce5.png?expires=1747842300&signature=d7b91606507680e56797ecb42b1b0a6e97d04d77e8d33a3fbeee96b1206f6ed3&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfX%2FMW1HO4zXAeXp7WA8uHmwRZZFVNyDUopofNQeik%2FaPAr0EZ%0AiBJv%2BoIoVZiuTIJW1wI%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946906/54a3eb2e08fc79d450b76a72c836/50ae7c12-0507-45c6-b926-678a8b54bce5.png?expires=1747842300&signature=d7b91606507680e56797ecb42b1b0a6e97d04d77e8d33a3fbeee96b1206f6ed3&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfX%2FMW1HO4zXAeXp7WA8uHmwRZZFVNyDUopofNQeik%2FaPAr0EZ%0AiBJv%2BoIoVZiuTIJW1wI%3D%0A)
## 2. Choose Your Desired Board
From the list of boards, pick the one you want to import. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946916/9388d8e451cb80d9b4be835bf35f/1bf7c414-fad1-41c7-a00f-d62d288bf151.png?expires=1747842300&signature=07e496d1af1264ea10190612234c35bc9eee7c041f0c6185e6aec1244110fd29&req=dSIkFsB6m4heX%2FMW1HO4zf9DdvpmTLjbRcT%2B0R%2FO0LWeSVBaawwlU%2BIV6GfG%0AL5u8ph%2BHn2%2BgaBQTbTM%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946916/9388d8e451cb80d9b4be835bf35f/1bf7c414-fad1-41c7-a00f-d62d288bf151.png?expires=1747842300&signature=07e496d1af1264ea10190612234c35bc9eee7c041f0c6185e6aec1244110fd29&req=dSIkFsB6m4heX%2FMW1HO4zf9DdvpmTLjbRcT%2B0R%2FO0LWeSVBaawwlU%2BIV6GfG%0AL5u8ph%2BHn2%2BgaBQTbTM%3D%0A)
## 3. Select the Kroolo Workspace
Click on **[Select Workspace](https://intercom.help/kroolo/en/articles/9772991-manage-workspaces)** to choose where the imported project will go in Kroolo.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946915/e0095059b015ad8f0c573eba7b52/923c3f03-1b4f-477a-b7d2-4f3fc574b397.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=4af96cd4baa1a16d9e28d0477f84be52defefeb2e6386b7489eae95f5ffdd611&req=dSIkFsB6m4heXPMW1HO4zS4yLCj6w9%2FnzynXFDo8mXmXSa%2B0sEkbJ72sdmEl%0A%2FaaImlmjH0fjSdQoHvI%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946915/e0095059b015ad8f0c573eba7b52/923c3f03-1b4f-477a-b7d2-4f3fc574b397.gif?expires=1747842300&signature=4af96cd4baa1a16d9e28d0477f84be52defefeb2e6386b7489eae95f5ffdd611&req=dSIkFsB6m4heXPMW1HO4zS4yLCj6w9%2FnzynXFDo8mXmXSa%2B0sEkbJ72sdmEl%0A%2FaaImlmjH0fjSdQoHvI%3D%0A)
## 4. Pick the Workspace
Select the appropriate Kroolo workspace for the [project](https://intercom.help/kroolo/en/articles/9795542-manage-projects-in-kroolo). 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946905/3ee1ea6d9c2e0ab2f0ccbe270cdd/89f11905-f48a-4163-8cb7-c33748818a94.png?expires=1747842300&signature=5149244b40a33ec816e58651f4c75c9b7e1dd2c6cc5713a1310148c35743f9cd&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfXPMW1HO4zXNEnT6ttHtuiZUeNYo9017AzX9uaM%2FmIYQxPzyd%0Au3iJcXwPRholJ5rU%2F4U%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946905/3ee1ea6d9c2e0ab2f0ccbe270cdd/89f11905-f48a-4163-8cb7-c33748818a94.png?expires=1747842300&signature=5149244b40a33ec816e58651f4c75c9b7e1dd2c6cc5713a1310148c35743f9cd&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfXPMW1HO4zXNEnT6ttHtuiZUeNYo9017AzX9uaM%2FmIYQxPzyd%0Au3iJcXwPRholJ5rU%2F4U%3D%0A)
## 5. Start the Import
Click on **Import** to begin transferring the board. 
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946913/5f4bfdb3f95678d00f3b2103d6d1/f823b584-7980-48dd-a9d2-55afbb419650.png?expires=1747842300&signature=9a7caf271b314f881a312ddbd1a8e70852b1b2adf1cf964405c49f346944c194&req=dSIkFsB6m4heWvMW1HO4zawZhFBVHO8IyRtuPCn%2By8dlqHXxWvnZN5maEaYU%0A02gbqjVMutcbxW3wa9k%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946913/5f4bfdb3f95678d00f3b2103d6d1/f823b584-7980-48dd-a9d2-55afbb419650.png?expires=1747842300&signature=9a7caf271b314f881a312ddbd1a8e70852b1b2adf1cf964405c49f346944c194&req=dSIkFsB6m4heWvMW1HO4zawZhFBVHO8IyRtuPCn%2By8dlqHXxWvnZN5maEaYU%0A02gbqjVMutcbxW3wa9k%3D%0A)
## 6. Review and Confirm
Review the list of items that will be imported, then click **Confirm** to complete the import.
[![](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946909/fb327831b23a9aef5c987c0e8a15/25ac142a-e617-45d0-8fd8-b4506257d6c0.png?expires=1747842300&signature=08bca2679c11f9311f919487d7c7ac556bd1f9e0690ca6947b5748a486bf374b&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfUPMW1HO4zUAQ2eP2rwAsplXcgdto0TYlWJxN%2Fm1vBtkUQM61%0AJdrCYR39TtzrcgWN7Nk%3D%0A)](https://downloads.intercomcdn.com/i/o/h4qkzypg/1230946909/fb327831b23a9aef5c987c0e8a15/25ac142a-e617-45d0-8fd8-b4506257d6c0.png?expires=1747842300&signature=08bca2679c11f9311f919487d7c7ac556bd1f9e0690ca6947b5748a486bf374b&req=dSIkFsB6m4hfUPMW1HO4zUAQ2eP2rwAsplXcgdto0TYlWJxN%2Fm1vBtkUQM61%0AJdrCYR39TtzrcgWN7Nk%3D%0A)
